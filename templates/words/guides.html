The basic Orb guide

The Orb is a hand gesture which can be performed in a group of any size or solo. The basic 'solo' orb is done by touch the tips ( ͡° ͜ʖ ͡°) of each finger on one hand to the same finger of the other hand. This includes the thumbs. Finally, the space inside the hands is turned into a sphere, or ORB, by extending and curling the fingers
